Clean Design

STŌR-e helps you build a Simple, Modern and Clutter-free online Store. It offers right amount of customisations options to make your Products (& Service offerings) standout. The result is an Easy- to-Use, modern website, designed for enhanced User Experience & conversions.

Easy Setup

We will manage all the work required to Setup up your Store. We will provide the creatives such Headers, Banners & other design elements. Ready to Go

STŌR-e includes all the essentials to process orders from day 1. All these and more with no paperwork whatsoever.

Store Management

We help in periodic monitoring of your store; answer customer queries, attend to complaints, book shipments, update stock levels, coordinate with logistics vendors for Status, process Cancellations, schedule reverse pickup etc. In short we will provide a personalised support to your customers just as you would.

Search Engine Optimization

We will implement On-Page SEO techniques by leveraging tools like Google Analytics to tune all aspects of your site like product descriptions, meta-information etc. This ensures your website is indexed correctly & increase the probability of appearing high on search engine ranking. The pages needs to be continuously optimized with targeted keywords to further improve/retain the rankings.

Brand Promotions

We will periodically publish content on your Social media pages & profiles and also create blog articles promoting your products. We will also let qualified products to be listed automatically in our associate site - MarketStreat, thereby opening another avenue for you to reach potential customers.
