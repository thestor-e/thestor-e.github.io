Features You'll Love.

STŌR-e's cloud platform helps the likes of Artists, Makers, Designers, Crafters to build an online identity for your brand. We take the effort off of you in Launching, Operating & Promoting your Online Store. Our unique package of Technology & Services frees you from the daily chores of managing the store and enables you to focus on your creative Work.

01.

Clean Design

STŌR-e helps you build a Simple, Modern and Clutter-free online Store. It offers right amount of customisations options to make your Products (& Service offerings) standout. The result is an Easy- to-use, Word class website, designed for enhanced User Experience.

02.

Easy Setup

We will manage all the work required to Setup up your Store. We will provide the creatives such Headers, Banners & other design elements. And, If you don't have a brand logo and need one, we will make it too. We will also optimize the product images to make it look the best & finally list the products online.

03.

Ready to Go

STŌR-e includes all business essentials to launch and carry out the transactions from day 1. It comes with a pre-integrated payment gateway, regulatory approvals to sell to overseas market, logistics support from reputed vendors. It provides multi Currency Support. All these and more with no paperwork whatsoever.

04.

Store Management

We help in periodic monitoring of your store to answer customer queries, attend to complaints, book shipments, update stock levels, coordinate with logistics vendors for Status, process Cancellations, schedule reverse pickup etc. In short we will provide personalised support to your customers just as you would.

05.

Search Engine Optimization

In order for your website to be indexed correctly & increase the probability to appear high on search engine ranking, the pages needs to be continuously optimized for related keywords. We will implement On-Page SEO techniques leveraging tools like Google Analytics to optimize all aspects of your site like product descriptions, meta-information etc.

06.

Brand Promotions

We will periodically publish content on your Social media pages & profiles and also create blogs promoting your products. We will also let qualified products to be listed automatically on our sister site - MarketStreat, thereby opening another avenue to reach potential customers.

Thoughtful Design

Clean & Organized pages

Create your portfolio + eCom site. Showcase your products to your potential customers in a layout that is visually inviting. Categorize your products use them as filter to enable easier navigation.
